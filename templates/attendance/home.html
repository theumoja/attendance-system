Architectural Overview

The Attendance Hub replaces legacy paper logs with an immutable tracking system. It bridges physical classroom attendance with analytical software by cross-referencing scheduled weekly timetables against active verification data. The platform registers student statuses in real-time, compiles localized telemetry, and outputs clean structured data streams for administrative audit logs.
